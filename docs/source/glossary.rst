Glossary
========

.. glossary::
   Battle Res (BR)
      Battle Res, refers to any spell that can resurrect another player in combat
   
   Bloodlust (BL)
      Bloodlust (in alliance old times was know in short term also known how **Hero**), refers to any spell that increase haste 30% all party/raid members for 40 seconds like `Bloodlust <https://www.wowhead.com/spell=282>`_, `Heroism <https://www.wowhead.com/spell=32182>`_, `Fury of the Aspects <https://www.wowhead.com/spell=390386>`_, `Time Warp <https://www.wowhead.com/spell=80353>`_ `Harrier's Cry <https://www.wowhead.com/spell=466904>`_

   Line Of Sight
      Line of sight means that either you can see the objective, or an ally or enemy can see you there is a "vision" or "visible" path to reach the target with an AOE or targeted spell. For example the common expression **Break LOS** means set your character behind wall or something to become not visible
